SuperFit Owner Portal Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SuperFit Owner Portal collects, uses, and protects information when gym owners use the platform to manage memberships, attendance, payments, and communication.

Information We Collect

We may collect account and business information provided by gym owners, including:

  • name, email address, phone number, and gym details
  • membership and attendance records entered by the gym owner
  • authentication data required to sign in securely
  • usage information needed to maintain and improve the platform

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • authenticate users and secure owner accounts
  • store and sync gym management data
  • support features such as attendance, payments, and communication tools
  • maintain service reliability and troubleshoot technical issues

Third-Party Services

SuperFit Owner Portal may rely on third-party infrastructure and authentication providers, including Supabase and Google Sign-In, to operate securely. Those services may process limited account information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect account and platform data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we aim to use standard safeguards appropriate for a modern web application.

Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce platform policies.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the SuperFit team through the business contact details associated with your deployment of the app.
